SECURITY POLICY

Our Commitment to Ethical Cybersecurity Practices

ETHICAL GUIDELINES

  • All security testing conducted with explicit written authorization
  • Strict adherence to responsible disclosure practices
  • No unauthorized access to systems or data
  • Compliance with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Protection of client confidentiality and data privacy

RESPONSIBLE DISCLOSURE

  • 90-day disclosure policy for vulnerabilities
  • Coordination with vendors and maintainers
  • Public disclosure only after patches are available
  • No exploitation of vulnerabilities for personal gain
